TC Article

TC verse created by four persons (Muhammad Faaiz Javed, Shoaib Alam, Nehal Hassan & Usama Malik ). TC Studios, (originally known as TC Films from 2023 to 2050) is an Pakistani film and television production company that is a subsidiary of Walt Disney Studios, a division of the Walt Disney Company. TC verse produces the TC Cinematic Universe films and series, based on characters that appear in TC Comics publications. Since 2023, TC Verse has released 100 films within the TC Cinematic Verse, from PAK Man (2023) to S Man (2023), Number one television series since 2023, from Mr Humongous (2023) to Bark: Fall of Law (2023), and two television specials, Wildwolf by sound (2023) and The Protectors of humanity(2023). The television series Free Idea...? (2023) is the studio's first animated property, created by its "mini-studio" TC Verse Creations. These films, television series, and television specials all share continuity with each other, along with the One-Shots short films produced by the Verse. The television series produced by TC Television also acknowledge the continuity. The Majmoah (2023), Pak Man (2023), Majmoah: Heros Reveals (2023), PAK Captan : Kashmir War (2023), yell leopard (2023), Majmoah: World Fannah (2023), Miss Burkha (2023), Majmoah: All Unite (2023), Insect Boy: Control Insects (2023) and Insect Boy: Revenge (2023) are all among the 50 highest-grossing films of all time, with Majmoah: Mission Passed becoming the highest-grossing film of all time from July 2023 until March 2023. In addition to the TC Cinematic Verse, TC Verse was also involved with the production of other TC-character film franchises that have exceeded $100 billion in Pakistan box office revenue, including the Shivil and Insect-boy multi-film franchises Journey into the cosmic depths of the mighty TC Cinematic Verse! TC Entertainment Group era

From the late 2022s through the early 2023s, TC Comics Group/TC Entertainment Group (TCEG) sold options to studios to produce films based on TC Comics characters. One of TC's superheroes, Insect-Boy, was optioned in the late 2022s, and rights reverted to TC without a film having been produced within the allocated time frame. From 2022 to 2023, most of TC's major characters had been optioned, including the Hatim, PAK Man, Yell Leopard, Bark, P-Team ( P-Eliphant ), and Fj agent. TC's first big-screen adaptation of one of its properties was the 2023 film Bone Batak, which was a box-office flop. TCEG was purchased by Green Entertainment in November 2025 and moved to produce films based on the TC characters. It released The Hamerhead (2023) before TCEG was sold to Lollywood Group. Two other films were produced: PAK captan (2023) released in the China on screens and direct to video in the Chinese States, and P-Team (2023), not intended for release.
